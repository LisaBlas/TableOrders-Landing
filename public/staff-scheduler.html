Staff Scheduler

Simple shift planning for restaurants, cafés, retail, and service businesses. No complexity, no bloat—just scheduling that works.

Visual calendar planning

See your entire month at a glance. Drag-and-drop shifts, spot gaps instantly, and adjust on the fly.

Employee availability tracking

Staff mark blocked days, system warns you before assigning conflicting shifts. No more scheduling mistakes.

Auto-schedule with balance

Distribute shifts fairly across your team. The system ensures everyone gets their target hours without overloading anyone.

Role-based scheduling

Assign shifts by role (cook, server, counter). System warns if you're missing coverage for critical positions.

Works on any device

Desktop, tablet, phone—your schedule is accessible anywhere. Local storage means it works offline too.

Why this works: Most scheduling software tries to do everything—payroll, HR, time tracking, compliance. You don't need that. You need a simple way to plan shifts and avoid conflicts. That's it.

Quick start: Add 2-3 employees with different roles, set their monthly shift targets, then click "Auto-Schedule" to generate a balanced calendar.
